# 我的楼简洁聚合页边界冻结单

## 1. Scope
- This addendum applies only to the current `profile` building surface.
- It freezes only:
  - the current role of `我的楼` as a compact current-user hub
  - the current visual-direction rule
  - the allowed relationship among personal profile, company detail, my-project entry, forum assets, and settings
  - the current explicit non-goals
- It does not by itself:
  - approve implementation completion
  - approve new contracts or backend schema
  - turn `profile` into a public profile surface
  - turn `profile` into a second forum homepage

## 2. Current Building Role
- `profile` remains:
  - the current actor's private or semi-private identity and asset center
- `profile` may now formally act as:
  - a compact hub page that aggregates bounded entry points from existing
    identity, organization, certification, membership, my-project handoff,
    forum assets, and settings
- `profile` does not become:
  - a public author homepage
  - a second forum browse tree
  - a generic consumer IM feature container

## 3. Visual Direction Rule
- The current visual direction for `我的楼` is frozen as:
  - concise
  - clean
  - grouped
  - WeChat-like in information density and rhythm
- This means:
  - one clear top profile summary area
  - grouped list-style entry sections
  - restrained copy and controlled iconography
  - no dashboard-like heavy card stacking as the dominant visual language
- This does not mean:
  - copying unrelated WeChat feature semantics into this app
  - importing social-IM concepts that do not belong to the current product truth

## 4. My-building Hub Boundary
- The normal `我的楼` page may aggregate only bounded entry summaries for:
  - personal profile
  - my company
  - certification or membership status
  - my project
  - my forum
  - settings
- The page should behave as:
  - an entry aggregation hub
  - a current-user summary surface
- The current first-level entry order is frozen as:
  - personal profile handoff through the top summary area
  - my company
  - certification or membership status
  - my project
  - my forum
  - settings
- `设置` should remain the bottom-most first-level entry family on the page.
- `我的项目` remains a bounded first-level handoff only.
- This handoff does not turn `profile` into the truth owner for:
  - project truth
  - order truth
  - contract truth
  - fulfillment truth
- The page must not behave as:
  - a second forum content home
  - a second workbench dashboard
  - a duplicate admin console

## 5. Personal Profile Boundary
- Tapping the avatar or profile summary may hand off into:
  - a personal profile page under the existing `profile` family
- The personal profile page in this package may keep only app-relevant fields and entry points.
- The personal profile page must explicitly remove:
  - `拍一拍`
  - `来电铃声`
- The previous consumer-IM style entry:
  - `我的发票抬头`
  must be replaced by:
  - `我的公司`
- `我的公司` must hand off into:
  - a company detail surface
  - still under the `profile` building family

## 6. Company Detail Boundary
- `我的公司` belongs to:
  - the current actor's organization or company identity surface
- It may discuss only:
  - company identity summary
  - current organization detail
  - bounded status or ownership information already allowed by profile truth
- It must not become:
  - a company-management console
  - an admin governance surface
  - a second organization-edit workflow unless separately frozen later

## 7. Settings Boundary
- Tapping `设置` may enter a richer settings page.
- The settings page in this package must stay app-native and app-relevant.
- Approved current settings families may include only bounded entries such as:
  - account and security
  - notifications
  - privacy and permissions
  - interface and display
  - general
  - storage
  - about
- The settings page must not drift into:
  - IM-only chat settings
  - audio-call product settings unrelated to this app
  - unrelated social-platform gimmick entries

## 8. Boundary With Forum Assets
- `我的楼` must aggregate forum personal assets through one bounded first-level entry only:
  - `我的论坛`
- Tapping `我的论坛` may hand off into a second-level forum personal asset surface.
- The second-level `我的论坛` surface may expose only:
  - `我的帖子`
  - `我的评论`
  - `我的收藏`
  - `我的关注`
  - `草稿箱`
- These remain:
  - actor-scoped asset entries
  - not a second forum browse home
- The top summary area and first-level page should not heavily duplicate detailed forum asset rows.
- Forum asset counts may appear only as:
  - a restrained top-summary hint
  - or a compact `我的论坛` subtitle summary
- They must not turn the first-level page back into:
  - a forum-heavy asset wall
  - a second forum index
- The profile building still must not own:
  - forum public browsing truth
  - forum publish truth
  - forum public author truth

## 9. Current Explicit Non-goals
- No new shell building
- No new bottom tab
- No public author homepage under `profile`
- No `拍一拍`
- No `来电铃声`
- No IM-product settings copy-paste
- No second forum homepage
- No admin-style organization management console
- No project / order / contract / fulfillment truth ownership transfer into `profile`
- No unapproved profile-private schema expansion by this file alone

## 10. Formal Conclusion
- Current formal conclusion:
  - `我的楼` is now frozen as a compact current-user hub rather than a forum-heavy management panel
  - avatar tap may enter a personal profile page under `profile`
  - `拍一拍` and `来电铃声` are outside the approved personal-profile surface
  - `我的发票抬头` is replaced by `我的公司`
  - `我的公司` may hand off into bounded company detail
  - `我的项目` may hand off from `我的楼` as a bounded first-level private-project entry
  - forum personal assets must collapse into one first-level `我的论坛` entry and expand only after handoff
  - `设置` must remain the bottom-most first-level entry family
  - `设置` may become richer, but only with app-native settings families
  - `profile` remains the entry owner for `我的项目` handoff only, not the project truth owner
- Current freeze type:
  - profile my-building compact hub boundary freeze only
